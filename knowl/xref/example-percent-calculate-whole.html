Example 2.1.7.

In a class 2 Alaska Native students make up 6.25% of the class. How many students are in the class?
Again we use the same setup, but we do not yet know the whole.
\begin{align*} \frac{\text{part}}{\text{whole}} & = \text{percent}.\\ \frac{2}{W} & = 0.0625.\\ \frac{2}{W} \cdot W & = 0.0625 \cdot W. \text{ Move } W \text{ out of the denominator}\\ 2 & = 0.0625 \cdot W.\\ \frac{2}{0.0625} & = \frac{0.0625 \cdot W}{0.0625}. \text{ Divide to isolate } W\\ \frac{2}{0.0625} & = W.\\ 32 & = W. \end{align*}
