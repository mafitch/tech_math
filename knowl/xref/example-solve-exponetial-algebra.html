Example 6.4.7.

Solve \(13 = 2+3e^{x-1}\text{.}\)
Solution.
Order of operations dictates the following
\begin{align*} 13 & = 2+3e^{x-1}.\\ 11 & = 3e^{x-1}.\\ \frac{11}{3} & = e^{x-1}\\ \ln\left(\frac{11}{3}\right) & = \ln\left(e^{x-1}\right).\\ \ln\left(\frac{11}{3}\right) & = x-1.\\ \ln\left(\frac{11}{3}\right)+1 & = x.\\ 2.299 & \approx x. \end{align*}
