Example 3.1.10.

Solve \(17-4y = 5+2y\text{.}\)
Solution.
\begin{align*} 17-4y & = 5+2y.\\ -5+17-4y & = -5+5+2y. & \text{Undo }+5\text{ by subtracting.}\\ 12-4y & = 2y.\\ 12-4y+4y & = 2y+4y. & \text{Bring terms with variable to the same side.}\\ 12 & = (2+4)y. & \text{ Factoring leaves only one variable.}\\ 12 & = 6y.\\ \frac{12}{6} & = \frac{6y}{6}. & \text{Undo multiplication with division.}\\ 2 & = y. \end{align*}
Notice we had to combine like terms (factor and add).
